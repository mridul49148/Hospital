About Us

Your Trusted Hospital
in Delhi NCR

Welcome to Sushila Hospital — a multi-speciality and trauma care centre trusted for delivering affordable, high-quality healthcare with compassion. We combine advanced medical technology with expert doctors and a patient-first approach.

From general consultations and diagnostics to specialised treatments and emergency care, we are committed to patient-centric services with excellence — recognised as one of the best hospitals in Delhi NCR.

🏥
Advanced Critical Care
18-bed ICU and Neonatal units with continuous monitoring and specialized care for adults and newborns.
🚑
Emergency & Maternity
Fully-equipped delivery suites and a dedicated emergency ward for urgent and maternity cases round the clock.
🔬
Expert Surgical Facilities
Three modern OTs with advanced C-Arm support for Urology, Ortho, Paediatrics, Gastro, and Neurosurgery.
